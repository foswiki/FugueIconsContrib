---++ Usage

This contrib contains a rather wonderful work of 2,757 icons made by Yusuke Kamiyamane that can be found at http://p.yusukekamiyamane.com/.

It contains not only 16x16 png icons, but also 48x48 source =.psd= files that can be used to extend, combine and customise.
